COACHING

The Benefits of Coaching

Coaching will enable the you to find answers from within yourself based on your values, preferences and unique perspective.

It is only when the you understand the results of specific behaviours that the process of change to affect a different set of results is possible. Let’s look at an example of when you may need a coach. Not everyone is happy in their job in fact, many whom I have spoken to are dissatisfied with their jobs. I have worked with many who pass the interview stage but find it difficult when taken on the job and worry about how they will cope. Well, we are advised to aim high and ‘go for it’ if this is you? contact me and I may be able to help.

Perhaps you are not sure anymore whether you wish to carry on in the job
perhaps you are worried your boss will find out you are not up to it.
Perhaps you have a boss who is intimidating and therefore you find it difficult settling in your new job

Maybe it is an old job and you find yourself with a new boss, don’t let anyone push you out of a job -call me.

Are you the manager, you’ve just been allocated a team of staff and you find there are a couple of difficult people within the team and you feel alone or just fed up you’ve been on training that is not helping? If this is you, I can help you deal with difficult people, and facilitate the realism of your dream. You might need someone acting as a coach, a mentor and perhaps even a counsellor, maybe you need to investigate your situation to see if there is anything about your personality that might be causing you to fail - it could be conflict, remember conflict is inevitable it’s how you deal with it that is the key issue!

What to expect

We will work together to look at areas in your life e.g. work , education , relationships , leisure , career family and develop a shared understanding of your needs, values and goals. We will work together to develop
skills and strategies to help you work towards achieving our goals consistent with your values and needs. You will be asked to undertake weekly
tasks between the sessions with a new to helping you act to enrich your life. We will work together to help you stay
focussed and develop resilience which will help you move forward and remove the blocks in your life. This will free you
from the repetitive and unhelpful thoughts and feelings that keep you stuck in the past.
